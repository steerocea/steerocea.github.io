Final Flame


About

Final Flame is a 2D platformer game that I developed using the Godot engine. This game was created within a team of 2 developers, including myself and one of my university class mates. We created this game in under 48 hours as part of the Game Jam Aotearoa 2023 .


We had to navigate the challenges of limited time, resources, and also a smaller team. We designed the gameplay mechanics, put together visuals, and engineered a polished user experience. Collaborating seamlessly with my teammate, I contributed my unique insights and problem-solving skills to deliver a cohesive and enjoyable experience.


This game's creation was my introduction to using Godot and also it's native language GDScript, which in term greatly improved my skillset.

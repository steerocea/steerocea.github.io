Breakout Evolution


About

Breakout Evolution is a reimagined version of the classic Breakout based around aa sci-fi and Vice City theme. Breakout Evolution's main new feature being power ups to enhance many different aspects of gameplay.


This game's was created over a roughly 4 week period as apart of my introduction to Computer Graphics and Games, meaning this was the first full game I had ever created. No engine was used instead everything was done inside Processing (as per assignment instructions). I have grown alot as a developer since this but I am proud to display this as my first ever game.
